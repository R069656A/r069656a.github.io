Cash Needed for HDB Resale

Have you secured your HDB/ bank loan but is still unclear on the cash portion that you need? This tool generates a breakdown report of all expenses including stamp duty(ies), legal fees, HPS Premium (if required) and miscellaneous fee. Loan-to-value, purchase price and valuation is taken into consideration to ensure accurate estimation.

55 and Above

Do you know what is your Full Retirement Sum (FRS)? With this tool, you can find out how much remaining refunds (if any) will be paid out to you in cash when you sell your flat after age 55.

Try it!
Interest Rate vs Purchase Price

My
Services

I will guide clients through every step, from assessing needs and market conditions to handling financing, home staging, and negotiations. I also provide investment consultations to identify lucrative opportunities, analyze market trends, and develop strategies. Property management consultations cover tenant acquisition, property maintenance, and financial management to maximize rental income.

Relocation consultations assist clients moving to a new area with neighborhood tours and coordinating home sales and purchases. For those looking to build, custom home building consultations help find land, connect with builders, and manage the construction process. These services showcase my dedication to supporting clients throughout their real estate journey.

My services include professional photography and videography to showcase properties with high-quality images and videos, listing properties on multiple platforms, and utilizing digital marketing strategies like targeted ads and email campaigns. I also organize open houses and virtual tours and promote properties on social media platforms to reach a broad audience.

Additionally, I provide staging and home presentation services to enhance the property's appeal, conduct market analysis to set competitive prices, and leverage professional networks to find buyers. I maintain regular client communication, providing updates on marketing efforts and buyer interest, and adjust strategies based on feedback. These comprehensive marketing services ensure maximum exposure and attract potential buyers, aiming to achieve the best sale price for the property.

I manage timelines by meticulously coordinating and scheduling every aspect of the buying or selling process. This includes setting up initial consultations, arranging property viewings, scheduling inspections, and ensuring all necessary paperwork is completed promptly. I utilize project management tools and maintain a detailed calendar to track deadlines, ensuring each step is completed on time. Regular communication with clients, inspectors, lenders, and other involved parties helps prevent delays and keeps the transaction moving smoothly.

In addition to managing appointments and deadlines, I proactively address potential issues that could impact the timeline. I stay informed about market conditions and any regulatory changes that might affect the process. By providing clients with clear expectations and regular updates, I help mitigate any unforeseen complications. This structured approach not only ensures that the transaction stays on track but also builds trust and confidence with clients, ensuring a seamless and efficient real estate experience.

After a purchase, I provide value by offering post-sale support and guidance to ensure a smooth transition for my clients. This includes assisting with the logistics of moving, recommending trusted service providers for renovations or repairs, and helping with utility setup and local service connections. I also provide valuable information about the neighborhood, such as schools, amenities, and community events, helping new homeowners settle in comfortably.

Additionally, I often remain a resource for clients long after the sale is complete. I offer advice on property maintenance, updates on market trends, and potential refinancing options. By keeping in touch through occasional check-ins, I maintain a lasting relationship, ensuring clients feel supported and valued. This ongoing engagement not only fosters client loyalty but also positions me as a go-to expert for future real estate needs.
